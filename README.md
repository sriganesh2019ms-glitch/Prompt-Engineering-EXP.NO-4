1. Comparative Analysis Prompt

Definition:

A Comparative Analysis Prompt is used to compare two or more items, technologies, or ideas by identifying their similarities, differences, advantages, and disadvantages. It helps in making better decisions by analysing multiple options.


Prompt:

Compare Smartphone A and Smartphone B in terms of battery life, AI camera features, processor performance, and price. Identify the advantages and disadvantages of both smartphones and recommend which one is better for daily use.
Output:

Smartphone A provides a 5000 mAh battery, which allows longer usage throughout the day, making it suitable for online classes and social media. Smartphone B has a 4500 mAh battery, but it supports faster charging.
In terms of AI camera features, Smartphone B has better night mode and AI scene detection, producing higher-quality images. Smartphone A has a good camera but fewer AI features.

For performance, Smartphone B has a more powerful processor which supports gaming and multitasking smoothly, while Smartphone A is suitable for basic tasks.

Considering price, Smartphone A is more affordable. Therefore, for students who prefer longer battery life and budget-friendly options, Smartphone A is recommended, while Smartphone B is better for users who prioritize camera quality and performance.

REAL LIFE EXAMPLE:

PROMPT:

Compare iPhone 17and Samsung Galaxy S24 in terms of battery life, AI camera features, processor performance, and price. Identify the advantages and disadvantages of both smartphones and recommend which one is better for daily use.

OUTPUT:
The Apple iPhone 17 offers efficient battery optimization with iOS and delivers smooth performance using the A19 processor. It provides excellent image quality with its 48 MP AI-powered camera and strong computational photography. The iPhone is known for its stable performance and seamless ecosystem.

The Samsung Galaxy S24 features a 4000 mAh battery, providing longer battery backup for daily activities. It includes a triple camera system (50 MP main camera) with advanced AI features such as zoom and scene optimization. The device is powered by the Exynos 2400 processor, which supports multitasking and gaming performance.

In terms of price, both smartphones are premium devices, but Samsung models may vary depending on configuration.
Overall, the iPhone 17 provides better performance optimization and software stability, while the Samsung Galaxy S24 offers more camera versatility and battery capacity.



2. Experiential Perspective Prompt

Definition:

An Experiential Perspective Prompt asks the AI to explain a concept from a specific person’s point of view, such as a student, teacher, engineer, or user. It focuses on personal experience and real-world usage.

Prompt:

From a college student's perspective, explain how a smartphone with long battery life, AI-powered camera, and smooth performance helps in managing daily activities such as online classes, communication, and capturing memories.

Output:

From a student’s perspective, a smartphone with long battery life is very helpful because it allows attending online classes, browsing study materials, and using learning apps without worrying about charging frequently.
The AI-powered camera helps capture clear photos of notes, assignments, and important moments with friends. AI features automatically improve lighting and focus.

Smooth performance ensures that apps such as Zoom, WhatsApp, Google Classroom, YouTube, and Instagram run without lag. This makes studying, communication, and entertainment easier for students.

Thus, smartphones with these features greatly improve productivity and convenience in daily student life.


5. Everyday Functioning Prompt

Definition:

An Everyday Functioning Prompt explains how a technology or concept is used in daily life. It focuses on practical applications that people experience regularly.

Prompt:

Describe how smartphone technologies such as AI cameras, long battery life, and powerful processors help users perform everyday activities like communication, photography, online learning, and entertainment.

Output:

Smartphones are widely used in everyday life for communication and productivity. Long battery life allows users to make calls, send messages, and attend online meetings throughout the day.

AI cameras improve photo quality by automatically adjusting brightness, focus, and scene settings, helping users capture better pictures easily.

Powerful processors enable smartphones to run multiple applications smoothly, such as social media, online learning platforms, and entertainment apps like YouTube and Netflix.

These features make smartphones essential for communication, education, productivity, and entertainment in modern daily life.
